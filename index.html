About & limits

This app keeps its own budget counter on your phone — it's separate from your bank balance. It cannot control whether BHIM (or any UPI app) uses UPI Lite or asks for a PIN; that's decided inside that app, not by this one. It also can't stop you from opening BHIM directly and skipping this gate entirely — it only works if scanning here is the habit you build. All data stays on this device; nothing is sent anywhere.
