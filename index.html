About & limits

This app keeps its own ledger on your phone — separate from your bank balance. It cannot control whether BHIM or any other app uses UPI Lite or asks for a PIN; that's decided inside that app, not this one. On iPhone, several UPI apps can register the same generic payment link, so "Default UPI app" may open something other than BHIM — use the named app buttons, or settle manually, when that matters. It also can't stop you from opening an app directly and skipping this gate — it only works if scanning here is the habit you build. All data stays on this device.
